Gingerbread Biscotti

Description

This spicy Italian gingerbread biscotti cookie recipe makes the perfect accompaniment for cocoa or coffee during the holiday season! For a special treat, serve with lemon curd or drizzle with lemon-flavored almond bark.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil
  • ¼ cup molasses
  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground ginger
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • ¾ tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ tablespoon ground cloves
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a cookie sheet.
  2. Combine sugar, eggs, oil, and molasses in a large bowl. Combine all-purpose flour, whole wheat flour, ground ginger, baking powder, ground cinnamon, ground cloves, and ground nutmeg in a separate bowl; stir into sugar mixture until a stiff dough forms.
  3. Divide dough in half; shape each into a roll the length of the cookie sheet. Place rolls on the prepared cookie sheet; pat down to flatten dough until ½-inch thick.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Set aside to cool.
  5. When cool enough to touch, cut into ½-inch thick diagonal slices.
  6. Place sliced biscotti on the cookie sheet; bake until toasted and crispy, 5 to 7 minutes on each side.
